# Rafia's Slides: Video Steganography

## Slide 1: What is Video Steganography?

Video steganography is the practice of hiding secret information inside a video file.

The video looks normal when played, but hidden data may be stored inside the file.

## Slide 2: How Does It Work?

Videos are made up of many frames.

Hidden data can be placed inside:

- Video frames
- Pixel changes
- Audio tracks
- Metadata

Because the changes are small, viewers usually cannot notice them.

## Slide 3: Why Is It Important?

Video steganography matters in cybersecurity because it hides information in plain sight.

It can be used for:

- Private communication
- Digital watermarking
- Protecting information

It can also be misused to hide stolen data or avoid detection.

## Slide 4: Main Takeaway

Cryptography makes information unreadable.

Steganography makes information invisible.

Video steganography uses normal-looking videos to hide secret data.
