# Rafia's Script: Video Steganography

My part of the project focuses on video steganography.

Video steganography is when secret information is hidden inside a video file. The video still looks normal when someone watches it, but hidden data can be stored inside different parts of the file.

This can be done through the video frames, small pixel changes, the audio track, or even the metadata of the file. Since videos contain many frames, small changes can be made without being obvious to the viewer.

In cybersecurity, this is important because it shows how information can be hidden in plain sight. It can be used for private communication or digital watermarking, but it can also be misused by attackers to hide stolen data or avoid detection.

The main idea is that cryptography makes information unreadable, while steganography makes information invisible.
